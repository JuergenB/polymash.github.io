🎨 About This Content Generator

This tool creates professional YouTube content for our First Friday exhibition series, featuring artists from our curated database of 5,000+ artists and 20,000+ artworks. It automatically generates:

  • Enhanced artist profiles with comprehensive website research
  • 3-minute YouTube scripts for full artist features
  • 1-minute YouTube Shorts scripts optimized for viral reach
  • Professional audio files ready for video production

All content is optimized for Not Real Art's brand voice and mission to amplify under-represented artists through our $2,000 grant program and monthly exhibitions.
